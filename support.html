怎么玩

  • 喷水擦拭:按住水晶表面并拖动,向接触处持续喷水冲刷,把灰尘一点点擦掉,露出下面光滑透明的水晶。想擦哪里就按哪里。
  • 旋转画面:拖动空白背景即可旋转,查看水晶的每一面。
  • 缩放:双指捏合缩放。
  • 日夜:点左上角 ☀️/🌙 在暖光与冷光之间切换。
  • 交付:擦净约 99% 后,右上角按钮会变成圆圈;点它交付,响起奖励音,之后只能旋转欣赏。
  • 重新开始:点右上角按钮(刷新/对勾)重新覆尘并重来。

How to play

  • Spray & wipe: press and drag on the crystal to spray water at the contact point, wiping the dust away bit by bit to reveal the smooth, clear crystal. Wipe wherever you like.
  • Rotate: drag the empty background to spin the crystal and view every face.
  • Zoom: pinch to zoom.
  • Day / night: tap the ☀️/🌙 button (top-left) to switch warm and cool lighting.
  • Deliver: once about 99% is clean, the top-right button turns into a circle; tap it to deliver — a reward chime plays and you can then rotate to admire it.
  • Restart: tap the top-right button (refresh / check) to re-cover with dust and start over.
