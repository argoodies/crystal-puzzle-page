故事Story

你是小镇上的解谜大师。近来镇上流言四起，你决定重新翻出箱底那盒尘封已久的水晶拼图。掀开盒盖，水晶早已布满厚厚的灰尘——你拿起喷壶，一点一点冲刷擦拭，让蒙尘的水晶重现通透光泽。随着灰尘褪去、光芒透出，那些流言背后的真相，也在你指尖一点点浮现…… You are the puzzle master of a small town. As strange rumors spread, you dig out a long-forgotten crystal puzzle from the bottom of your chest. Lifting the lid, you find it buried under thick dust — so you take up your spray and wash it clean, bit by bit, until the crystal's clear glow returns. As the dust falls away and the light shines through, the truth behind the rumors slowly surfaces beneath your fingertips…

简介About

开局是一枚覆满灰尘的拼图形水晶原石。按住并拖动手指，向接触处喷水冲刷，一点点擦掉表面的灰尘，露出下面光滑透明、会折射光芒的水晶。当擦干净约 99%，即可「交付」——听一声清脆的奖励音，再欣赏你打磨好的宝石。全程在你的设备上运行。 You start with a dust-covered, puzzle-shaped rough crystal. Hold and drag to spray water at the contact point, wiping the dust away bit by bit to reveal the smooth, translucent, light-refracting crystal underneath. Clean about 99% to "deliver" it — a bright reward chime plays — then admire the gem you polished. Everything runs on your device.
